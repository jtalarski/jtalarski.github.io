Recent Development Projects

watchUwatch is a full-stack application that allows a group of invited users (aka friends) to share lists of streaming media that interest them. The goal for the app is to keep a group of friends apprised of each other’s current streaming perferences for purposes of finding new media to watch themselves and to generate group discussion.

Users who log into the app will be able to browse for new movies to add to their queue, update the status of their movie watching from “queued”, or “watching” through to “watched” as well as to delete older movies. Users will also be able to view other users’ movie queues with read only access.
